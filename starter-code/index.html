Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso, or Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid facedown on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Why Memory Games

Everyone knows that Games are a great way to pass the time, but it may come as a surprise to some that games can help you in your everyday life. Memory games, for example, are great ways to improve focus and attention to details. Since this can lead to improvement in your work (and that in turn can lead to more money!), take a moment and have some fun while working on your future!
